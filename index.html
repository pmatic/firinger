about

Margareta Firinger was born in 1994 in Zagreb, Croatia. Her first dance steps and theatre performances were realized in the dance studio "Lepeza VV". Through high school she was involved in dance, acting, and music. In 2009, she enrolled at the School of Contemporary Dance Ana Maletić where she gradually devoted herself exclusively to contemporary dance. After graduation, she enrolled at the Salzburg Experimental Academy of Dance (SEAD).

In SEAD, she had the opportunity to work with teachers and choreographers such as Helder Seabra, Julyen Hamilton, Milla Koistinen, Lali Ayguadé, Eléonore Valère, Eduardo Torroja, Meytal Blanaru, and many others. Margareta has been a member of the Zagreb Dance Company since 2019. In addition to working in the Company, she is actively working on her own smaller projects.
